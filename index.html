Self-Control

And how to improve

What I did wrong:

  • I watched without my parents permission
  • I watched while hiding it from my parents
  • I watched for too long during exam times

How To Fix:

  • I should keep the devices away from me
  • I should hide the devices when not using them
  • I should remove the thought of trying to watch without permission

What to do going into the future:

  • Maintain the same principals as mentioned before
  • Make those principals a habit
  • And not lose control again by keeping away the thoughts
  • Always measure the time and set timers while watching/playing




This is what I can do get self-control.
